Kids teaching kids

At Koch Skole we engage children in the core of the educational system by giving them a chance to be a part of the teaching process. We convey this idea by mixing the classes and organizing the lectures that they need to be taught. This way we spur the kids imagination while keeping them interested in the subject they are learning.

Recess

Recess is a highly influential part in everyday life of a schoolchild. What makes it exceptional is that they are the ones initiating the bonding, exchanging culture and most importantly doing all of this through playing. Playground is a place where friendships start and grow.

Come and join the fun!
